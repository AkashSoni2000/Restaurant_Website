About Us

We are your one-stop destination for delicious Indian cuisine. I am gaining experience as chefs were behind the name and flavor. They began to consider the idea three years ago, and it became a reality in August of 2022. Since then, a slew of celebrities has come to sample the spice of the Indian cuisine. Our restaurant and Chef-Owner are incredible, and their outstanding Indian food culture and spice blends consistently delight their customers. Our restaurant is frequented by many Indian celebrities.

Our core team enjoyed tasting the various spices, and we all spent time looking for desi Indian food so that we could enjoy the excellent food. We realized after spending too much time that we were not the only ones who missed the authentic taste and street food options, so we decided to serve everyone and dreamed up the name Gudiya Restaurant.
